Intersting Facts about the Chameleon


  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Chainging skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches


Intersting Facts about the Pigeon


  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities


Intersting Facts about the Camel


  • Camel's ears are furry
  • Camels can move easily across the sand because of it's specially designed feet
  • When camels find water, they will drink as much as possible since they live in very dry areas


Intersting Facts about the Cicada


  • Cicadas are among the loudest insects known to man
  • A swarm of cicadas can produce sounds up to 120 decibels
  • Periodical cicadas have 13 and 17 year life cycles
